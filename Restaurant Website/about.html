About us

Our Story

Since our conception in 2019, a group of friends have been bringing the joy of mouthwatering Mexican cuisine to the people of Ottawa.

Taking our love of fresh-baked tortillas, tacos, and tangy salsas, we have decided to create the perfect balance of decor and flavour, right in the heart of downtown Ottawa.
